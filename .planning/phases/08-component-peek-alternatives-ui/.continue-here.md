Phase 8 decisions that must survive resume:

- Peek content should be almost entirely visual. At most, show a tiny title/name above the visual.

- Current route/sidebar groupings are too broad and inconsistent; expand useful category/tag/type vocabulary where current data supports it.
- Long sidebars and pill lists must become scrollable instead of stretching the page.
- Remove or strongly demote noisy labels such as `verified item` and `high confidence` from normal browsing UI.
- Keep useful actions: `View component`, profile/detail access, Docs, Registry homepage, and copy-only commands where appropriate.
- Related/similar components should appear as a small navigator/strip on item/peek detail surfaces, not inside every discovery card.
- Related browsing is a foundation for future production pattern upgrades, but Phase 8 must not claim `better`, `best`, `correct`, `polished`, or `production-grade` without real evidence.
