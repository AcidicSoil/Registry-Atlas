Summary
-------

The shadcn/ui community registry includes a growing ecosystem of third‑party component libraries. Libraries like **Basecn**, **Coss UI**, **Cult UI**, **Dice UI**, **Aceternity**, **ABUI** and **ElevenLabs UI** provide their own spin on standard shadcn primitives, while **BillingSDK** and **Animate UI** offer specialized collections (billing UIs and animated components respectively). Meanwhile, larger providers like **Creative Tim UI** host an extensive registry of components and blocks with CLI installation. Other registries in the directory are primarily block collections or niche tools and were skipped per the user’s criteria.
